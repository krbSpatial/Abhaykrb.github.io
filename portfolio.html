Rainfall Distribution

This was a research project that I did for Indian researchers that were on research related to water quality of wells in Andhra Pradesh region. It was a hectic project where extensive data cleaning was needed to convert excel sheet data to working shapefile data format. I used interpolation technique to generate rainfall distribution maps for years 2020, 2021 & 2022. I also had to prepare chlorine, potassium, Co3AsCaCO3 distribution for pre and post monsoon for all those respective years. Since the workflow was quite repeatative I had prepared a model using QGIS model builder that eased my job.
